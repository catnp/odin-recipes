Raspberry Jam Recipe

Description

A simple recipe to produce a tangy and sweet confiture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raspberries
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a large pot.
  2. Throughly crush raspberries. Stir together the crushed fruit and sugar and lemon juice until you have a thick slurry.
  3. Turn on heat to medium-high, and bring to a boil, stirring slowly. Continue to crush fruit as it heats to encourage it to release its water.
  4. Remove jam from heat, and transfer to clean jam jar(s). Allow to cool to room temp before capping and storing in the fridge!
